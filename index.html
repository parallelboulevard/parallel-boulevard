Parallel Boulevard

Indie Rock from the North Sea Coast

About

Parallel Boulevard is an indie rock band inspired by the sea breeze, cloudy skies, and coastal roads of Noordwijk, Netherlands. Our music blends melancholic melodies with energetic rhythms — like a storm rolling in from the North Sea.
